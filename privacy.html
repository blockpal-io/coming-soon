Blockpal Privacy Policy

At Blockpal, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the limited information we collect and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

Blockpal does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details.

2. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Blockpal does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies for marketing purposes or any other reason.

3. Sharing Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not share any data with third-party services or partners.

4. Data Handling for All Users

Blockpal handles data uniformly across all users, regardless of geographic location. However, since we do not collect personal data, this applies only to non-personal technical data.

5. Data Security

As Blockpal does not collect personal data, there are no specific security measures required for protecting personal information.

6. Data Retention

Blockpal does not retain any personal data since none is collected.

7. User Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no options for users to opt out of data collection, request data deletion, or manage any personal data.

8. Communications

Blockpal does not send marketing emails or any other form of unsolicited communication.

9. Compliance with Legal Obligations

Blockpal is not subject to specific legal obligations regarding personal data collection (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), as we do not collect any personal data.

10. Data Breach Notifications

Since no personal data is collected, Blockpal does not have a procedure in place for notifying users of data breaches.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
